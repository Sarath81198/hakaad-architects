Principal Architect

Harish

To be an architect was his childhood aspiration. Ar. Harish completed his architecture degree in ACE, Hosur and established HaakaD.

His passion for design led him to work across Chennai, Hyderabad, and Bangalore, shaping a distinct design language rooted in both global ideas and local context.

An active member of IIA, he collaborates with designers and artisans to deliver thoughtful, detailed architectural experiences.
